My journey has been slow and steady, I rely specifically on client referrals.   I’m not big on promoting myself via social media or other online advertising.   I decided to invest in myself rather than working out of a box gym, some of that choice was mandatory as Covid restrictions limited my options.  I am visible in the community, training clients at parks, my house (nice gym setup), or in the comfort of their own homes.   I have been successful with client retention and offer lots of different services.  

It’s an enjoyable profession and very rewarding.   The program at Mesa helped inspire the confidence with practical applications and superior knowledge to provide stellar training.   Looking forward to the growth I have experienced both in business and as an ever learning student.   Remain coachable!
