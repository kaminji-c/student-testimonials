Personal Trainer Program in San Diego Mesa College was one of the best decision that I could have made. I have been working out for nearly 18 years and during this time I have heard so much conflicting health and fitness advice from trainers. Honestly I never knew if what I was told to people I was helping was 100 % accurate. But at the same time I truly wanted to help people breakthrough their biggest obstacles.

Learning so much about the proven science behind everything was priceless. I left the Program feeling confident in the fact that I proudly could call myself an educated fitness trainer.
